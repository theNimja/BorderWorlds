story



    In the years of 13337,Humanity found it's place among the stars,exploring star systems eveywhere.
    The frontiers of human exploration are known as the Border Worlds.

    Virtually uncharted, unexplored, and untouched, these areas represent some of the best resource gathering oppurtunities.

    However, this is not without it's fair share of dangers.Uncleared asteroid fields,unknown alien organisms...Worst, a man who cannot make credits is a man who cannot survive.

    You are one of these brave traders, seeking to make your fortune in the Border Worlds,braving the dangers.Will you survive? Will you become rich? Will you become the most prosperous of ... The Traders of the Border Worlds?
