    In the years of 3l337,Humanity found it's place
    among the stars,exploring star systems everywhere.

    The frontiers of human exploration are known as the
    Border Worlds.

    Virtually uncharted, unexplored, and untouched, these
    areas represent some of the best resource gathering
    opportunities.

    However, this is not without it's fair share of dangers.
    Uncleared asteroid fields, ruthless pirates, unknown
    alien organisms... Worst, a man who cannot make
    credits is a man who cannot survive.

    You are one of these brave traders, seeking to make
    your fortune in the Border Worlds,braving the
    dangers.Will you survive? Will you become rich?
    Will you become the most prosperous of ...
    The Traders of the Border Worlds?



    Click to tell the ship where to move to. If you move
    close to a planet,you will dock with it,and be able to
    purchase and sell resources.You can also repair your
    ship with the "Repair" button.



    Traders of the Border Worlds is a Space trading
    game where you fly around space in your space ship
    and trade resources with the planets you pass. You
    need to make sure that you don't buy or sell to many
    resources to a single planet or their economy will
    change making things harder for you.

    P.S. the red ship is a pirate who will steal your credits.
